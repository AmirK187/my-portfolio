About me

I am a Diesel Artisan by trade.
Grew up in Kimberley, Northern Cape.
I started my Diesel apprenticeship in 2013 in Johannesburg, qualified in 2015.
Worked across South Africa, having the privilege to explore and discover the beautiful country side.
In 2016 I had been introduced to the tech world whilst working in Johannesburg.
This became an idea in the back of my mind for a career change.
Finally in 2018 I started paying more attention to the world of development.
By 2019 I had learnt HTML/CSS with SCSS, Bootstrap and vanilla JavaScript.
I've also been putting in the hours to learn Node.js alongside the React Framework.
My goal is to become a awesome Front-End Web developer and transition to Fullstack.
